About Us

VXN wasn't born in a boardroom. It started during a random call late at night between friends. Just a name, just a spark. But sparks have a way of becoming something bigger.

From that moment the vision expanded. VXN means Vision Exceeds Now — the belief that the future is being built right now by those bold enough to see it.
